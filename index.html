About

A MULTIDISCIPLINARY HACKATHON TO HELP PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES

The goal of ATHack is to bring awareness to the important field of developing assistive technologies. We pair teams of students with clients in the Boston/Cambridge community who live with a disability. Each client has a problem in mind which they face because of their disability. Over the course of the hackathon, students brainstorm, design, and create prototype solutions for their client, making a huge difference in their lives.
